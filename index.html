Privacy Policy

Comp Decoder — Android Application

Last updated: February 28, 2026

The Short Version

Comp Decoder does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All compensation data you enter stays on your device. We do not track you, show ads, or sell your data. Ever.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Comp Decoder ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Comp Decoder Android application. The App is developed and maintained by Samir Saad.

By using Comp Decoder, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Comp Decoder is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Compensation figures, salary data, or offer details you enter into the App
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data beyond what Google Play automatically provides to all developers
  • Any financial account information

3. Data You Enter Into the App

All compensation data, offer details, and negotiation information you input into Comp Decoder is processed entirely on your device. This data:

  • Is never transmitted to our servers (we have none)
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • May be stored locally on your device using your device's standard storage (SharedPreferences or localStorage) solely to preserve your session
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App
Plain language: The salary numbers you type in stay on your phone. Nobody else sees them — not us, not Google (beyond standard Play Store operations), not anyone.

4. Google Play Billing

Comp Decoder offers optional paid features (Pro and Premium) through Google Play's in-app billing system. When you make a purchase:

  • All payment processing is handled exclusively by Google Play
  • We do not receive or store your payment card information
  • We receive only a confirmation from Google that a purchase was made, which is used solely to unlock the features you purchased
  • Google's handling of payment data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media SDKs, or any third-party data collection services.

The App loads fonts from Google Fonts over the internet when an internet connection is available. This request is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No personal data from you is included in this request.

6. Children's Privacy

Comp Decoder is intended for adults (18+) who are engaged in employment negotiations. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data stored on our end to secure. Any data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to delete, export, or correct on our end. You can remove all locally stored app data at any time by:

  • Going to your device's Settings → Apps → Comp Decoder → Storage → Clear Data
  • Or by uninstalling the App

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, please contact:

  • Developer: Samir Saad
  • Email: privacy@compdecoder.app
  • App: Comp Decoder (Google Play)

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.
